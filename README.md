# Best-Heritage-Resort-Stays-for-Relaxing-Getaways-Near-Pune-1
Some weekends aren’t meant for malls or long to-do lists. They’re meant for slow mornings, fresh air, and that peaceful feeling you only get when you’re away from the city noise. If you’re in Pune and looking for a relaxing escape without traveling too far, heritage-style resorts are honestly a perfect choice.
They don’t just offer rooms — they offer space, calmness, and a break from routine.
Here are some of the best heritage resort stays near Pune where you can truly unwind.

🌿 The Grand Heritage Resorts
If you’re looking for a peaceful property with open green lawns and a classic heritage feel, this is a lovely option. The atmosphere here feels relaxed from the moment you enter. Wide spaces, traditional-inspired architecture, and calm surroundings make it ideal for a quiet weekend.
It’s the kind of place where you can:
•	Sip chai on the lawn in the evening
•	Take long, slow walks without traffic noise
•	Spend quality time with family without distractions
It’s not rushed, not crowded — just simple, calm, and comfortable.

🏰 Fort JadhavGADH
If you enjoy history and heritage vibes, this fort-turned-resort gives you a very different experience. The old stone walls, traditional décor, and peaceful courtyards make it feel like stepping into another era.
It’s quiet in a beautiful way. Early mornings here feel special — cool breeze, birds chirping, and that old-world charm all around you. Perfect for couples or anyone who wants something beyond a regular hotel stay.

🌄 Sunny's World
For those who love open views and fresh air, Sunny’s World offers a hill-facing setting that feels refreshing. The wide spaces and greenery give you room to breathe — literally.
It’s ideal if your idea of relaxation is:
•	Watching sunsets
•	Enjoying quiet outdoor dinners
•	Waking up to natural light instead of alarms
Simple comfort with a scenic touch.

🌳 The Corinthians Resort & Club
This property blends luxury with calm surroundings. The architecture has a classic touch, while the greenery around keeps things peaceful. It’s a great choice for a slightly more upscale, comfortable stay without losing that relaxed vibe.
You can easily spend a full day here doing nothing — and that’s the best part.

⛳ Oxford Golf Resort
Surrounded by open golf greens, this resort has a very serene atmosphere. The views are wide and uncluttered, which instantly makes you feel lighter.
It’s not about activities or loud entertainment — it’s about space, silence, and slowing down.

Why Heritage Resorts Feel More Relaxing
There’s something about heritage-style properties that feels warmer and more personal. Maybe it’s the architecture. Maybe it’s the slower pace. Or maybe it’s just the way these places are designed — with open courtyards, greenery, and peaceful corners.
Unlike busy city hotels, these resorts encourage you to:
•	Disconnect from constant notifications
•	Spend real time with your loved ones
•	Enjoy nature without rushing
And sometimes, that’s all we really need.
